Pig dice Game!!

Rules of the game: Two player game, start by roling the dice! If you score 2-6 add the number on the die to your score. Between each role you have the option of role again or hold. If you score a 1 then it is the next players turn and no score is to be tallied

To begin, please enter your name and click start to begin.
